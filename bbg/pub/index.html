About:

Baskets by Gayle has been creating gourmet gift baskets for over twenty five years, and our reputation has been built upon mouthwatering delicacies and personalized attention. Baskets by Gayle’s product line is ever-changing and designed to please a wide variety of tastes. Our homemade specialties set us apart from anything that Philadelphia has to offer. Word of mouth, from our satisfied customers, and the baskets themselves has always been our most effective testimonials. We were honored to have been awarded

Philadelphia Magazine’s BEST OF PHILLY

designation as offering the finest gift baskets in the city. But it does not end there… Our creative staff is available to lend their expert advice to assure that your baskets are the ones that will always be remembered. We provide local delivery and UPS Nationwide delivery. We accept all major credit cards. Custom gift packages start at $45, but we offer gifts starting at $25. We appreciate your patronage and will do our best to exceed your expectations. Please call us so that we may answer any questions about your gift giving needs.
