Hello World!

I have been working in Philadelphia restaurants for over ten years now. I started by cutting vegetables and cleaning in a kitchen.
Eventually I moved into a line cook position in an up and coming bar. During that time I was also a personal chef, teacher's assistant at my school, and a meat slicer in a top restaurant.
When I received my first serving job in Philadelphia I left my other four jobs to work full time. Just after a year of serving I became a Manager, then Beverage Manager, then eventually General Manager.
I had the pleasure of working for great people all over the city and what I learned will only help me for the future.
